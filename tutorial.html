Tutorial

This is Monospace, the minimal writing and notes replacement app for Android.

  • Monospace keeps things minimal, allowing for basic formatting using its designed-for-touch UI. Simply highlight the text you'd like to format.

  • You can keep all your devices in sync by signing in with Dropbox.

  • Keep things organised by using Monospace's modern tagging system, simply add tags to the final line of the file, we'll handle the rest.

  • Monospace allows you to export in plain text or Markdown, meaning it has copy and paste support with online services such as wordpress.
